Fractal Generator

Welcome to my Fractal Generator! This app allows you to create your own fractals by just modifying a few settings. The controls on the "Sandbox" page control how the canvas draws the fractals to the screen and how the fractals are generated by the app. If you choose one of the fractals I have premade for you, the controls on the screen will be updated to show you what settings are used for it as a starting point for you if you decide you would like to try creating your own custom fractals. Finally, you are able to move each vertex of the shapes independently or move the vertex in the center to move the whole shape. When you're ready, head to the "Sandbox" page to get started! I hope you enjoy!
